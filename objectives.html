Our Objectives

Future of Provid

Next Generation Of Development

Prodiv Real Estate Company intends to be the leading developer of real estate projects in Nigeria. Initially , the focus wil be on the Abuja were huge growth is expected over the coming years due to mass mirgration of people from the rural parts of the country.We will then rapidly expand througout the Country to meet the demand for residential units in other upcoming cities in Nigeria, with an increase in the housing deficit from 17 million homes as at 2012 to 22 million homes in 2021, there lies a formidable challenge in which we are eager to engage.

Our Scope Of Business

Acquisition of raw with the potential to develop distinctive, residential developments, oferring different types of housing and urban environments, aimed at redefining lifestyle in Nigeria.
Development of landmark looking to the future and meeting the demands of the next generation.
Creation of broader , large scale entertaiment and tourism projects to be ran and managed by sepcialist international leisure operators.
Establishment and management of Real Estate Investment Funds (REITS ) in accordance with Nigeria regulatory framework.
Provision of real estate services including property management, real estate marketing and real estate valuation, with selected specialized partners.

Motivated By Inspiration

The story begins with a group of young succesful business men from Nigeria, recongnizing the market needs and subsequently launching a company to aid homes buyesr in the country.The shareholders actively contribute to the construction and development of Provid's various on going projects across Abuja. Combining their inspired innovation and innate understanding of the market, Prodiv will deliver projects integrated to the region's tradition but mindful of the needs and desire for creativity and modernity. Looking to the future, the Chairman and the Board of Directors are committed in making prodiv the leading community developer throughout the country, not just in Abuja. With a team of specialized talented professionals, Prodiv looks to lead the market in pioneering, landmark projects.
